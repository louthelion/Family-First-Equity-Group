Organize what is known.

Family First Equity Group can help organize known property information and prepare questions for the appropriate attorney, title professional, tax professional, or licensed advisor.

Possible information

  • Property addresses and current use
  • Known deeds, tax records, insurance, and loans
  • Condition, occupancy, and maintenance
  • Known decision-makers and family goals
  • Questions requiring professional guidance

Do not upload or submit confidential legal documents, account numbers, Social Security numbers, or private credentials.
