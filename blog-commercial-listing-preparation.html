Commercial Listing Preparation Tip

A focused checklist for organizing accurate commercial property information before public presentation.

Blog Post summary

A focused checklist for organizing accurate commercial property information before public presentation.

Lead with verifiable facts

Use the correct address, property type, current use, approximate size with source, occupancy status, and access process. Do not present estimates as certified measurements.

Explain the space clearly

Describe layout, parking, utilities, loading, visibility, equipment, and known improvements without making unsupported compliance or condition claims.

Use current media

Provide level, well-lit exterior and interior photographs. Label dates and avoid old images that no longer represent the property.

Prepare supporting questions

Identify what remains to be verified about zoning, leases, expenses, title, condition, environmental matters, and availability.

Listing checklist

Verify authority; date all information; label estimates; remove private data; use recent photos; and have qualified professionals review statements within their expertise.
