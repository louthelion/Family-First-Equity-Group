What we do

Family First Equity Group is a real estate investment company. We evaluate and acquire assets based on internal investment criteria — with a primary focus on commercial real estate, multi-family, business centers, plazas, and value-driven opportunities. We also review select residential and land opportunities when the deal fits.

We are not a real estate brokerage and do not represent buyers or sellers as an agent. We review opportunities as a principal investor.

Primary focus (commercial-first)

  • Listed commercial assets (retail, office, mixed use)
  • Multi-family (small to mid-size)
  • Plazas / business centers
  • Land (strategic / value-driven)
  • Select residential (when the opportunity fits our criteria)
