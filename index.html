About Family First

Family First Equity Group is a private real estate investment company. We buy assets directly and focus primarily on listed commercial properties, multifamily assets (including smaller apartment buildings), business centers/plazas, land, and select residential opportunities when the deal fits.

We evaluate opportunities across different situations — including distressed, bank sale, off-market, or standard transactions — and we structure deals based on the property, the numbers, and the timeline. Our goal is to acquire, improve operations when needed, and build value over time.

Notice: Family First Equity Group is not a real estate brokerage and does not provide real estate agent services. We do not provide legal or tax advice. Submissions through this portal are for deal review and internal evaluation only.
