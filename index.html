About Family First

Family First Equity Group is a private real estate investment company focused primarily on acquiring listed commercial properties, multifamily assets, business centers (plazas), land, and strategic residential opportunities. Our priority is listed commercial — but we review any strong deal (direct purchase, bank sale, off-market, etc.).

We purchase assets directly. We are not a real estate brokerage and we do not provide legal or real estate advice. Submissions are for deal review and internal evaluation only.
