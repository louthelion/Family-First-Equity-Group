Learning Center/Short-Term Rental / Airbnb Basics
Foundational lesson

This lesson offers a practical starting point for organizing property information and preparing informed questions.

Confirm whether the use is allowed

Review local ordinances, zoning, permits, licenses, taxes, HOA or condominium rules, lease restrictions, and occupancy requirements.

Assess guest and property safety

Organize access, emergency information, smoke and carbon monoxide devices, stairs, pools, parking, security, cleaning, and maintenance for professional review.

Plan the operation

Define booking, communication, turnovers, supplies, repairs, neighbor concerns, recordkeeping, and who can respond when the owner is unavailable.

Model variable income carefully

Account for seasonality, vacancy, platform fees, cleaning, taxes, utilities, maintenance, insurance, furnishings, and reserves. Revenue is not guaranteed.

Know when to bring in a professional

Property decisions may involve legal rights, taxes, title, financing, insurance, inspections, permits, valuation, or brokerage. Use qualified professionals for advice, verification, and decisions in those areas.
