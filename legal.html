Role and services

Family First Equity Group reviews property opportunities and helps organize information for real estate opportunity review, property transition, management, short-term rental, and legacy property organization.

Family First Equity Group is not a real estate broker, real estate agent, lender, attorney, tax advisor, financial advisor, title company, probate professional, appraiser, insurance professional, or estate planning professional. We do not represent a visitor in a licensed professional capacity.

No guarantees

Family First Equity Group provides education, organization, and property opportunity review. We do not provide legal, tax, probate, title, brokerage, lending, or financial advice. We do not guarantee purchase, sale, listing approval, management acceptance, rental income, occupancy, cash flow, appreciation, financial outcome, or any specific result. Visitors should consult the appropriate licensed professional for legal, tax, title, probate, lending, or financial decisions.

Submissions and website information

A form submission does not create a contract, agency relationship, fiduciary relationship, offer, listing agreement, financing commitment, management agreement, or promise to proceed. Website information is general and may not reflect all facts relevant to a property. Property details and opportunities require independent verification and due diligence.
